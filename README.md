# 🎬 Fabrizio Santini - Premium Actor Portfolio

A cinematic, bilingual portfolio website for Mexican actor Fabrizio Santini, showcasing his impressive career spanning voice acting, television, and film.

## 🌟 Live Website
**[https://bayouwebstudio.github.io/fabriziosantini/](https://bayouwebstudio.github.io/fabriziosantini/)**

## ✨ Features

### 🎭 Comprehensive Portfolio
- **Complete Filmography**: From his breakthrough voice work in "La leyenda de la Nahuala" (2007) to his starring role in Amazon Prime's "How to Survive Being Single"
- **Career Timeline**: 20+ years in entertainment, including work with Netflix ("Narcos: Mexico") and Telemundo ("Lady of Steel")
- **Awards Recognition**: Ariel Award nomination for "Leaf Blower" (2016)

### 🌎 Bilingual Experience
- **Spanish/English Toggle**: Seamless language switching with localStorage preference saving
- **Cultural Authenticity**: Content crafted to honor his Mexican heritage while appealing to international audiences

### 🎨 Cinematic Design
- **Dark Elegance**: Deep charcoal backgrounds with warm copper/gold accents (#c4873a)
- **Movie Poster Aesthetic**: Inspired by theatrical one-sheets and film marketing
- **Smooth Animations**: Parallax effects, scroll animations, and fluid transitions
- **Premium Typography**: Cinzel serif for headlines, Inter for body text

### 📱 Technical Excellence
- **Fully Responsive**: Optimized for all devices from mobile to 4K displays
- **Performance Optimized**: Throttled scroll events, lazy loading, efficient animations
- **Zero Dependencies**: Pure HTML/CSS/JavaScript for maximum performance
- **SEO Ready**: Semantic HTML structure and meta optimization

### 🚀 Interactive Features
- **Smart Navigation**: Smooth scrolling with active section highlighting
- **Filterable Filmography**: Sort by film, TV, voice acting, or view all
- **Image Gallery**: Professional stills from his major productions
- **Social Integration**: Direct links to Instagram (@aquelsantini), Twitter, and IMDb

## 🏗️ About Fabrizio Santini

Born into a family of performers, Fabrizio Santini has established himself as one of Mexico's most versatile actors:

- **Family Legacy**: Son of Love Santini, nephew of Loretta Santini, brother of Brandon Santini
- **Early Start**: Began voice acting at age 8 in 2002
- **Breakthrough**: Voiced Leo San Juan in the beloved animated film "La leyenda de la Nahuala"
- **Film Recognition**: Ariel Award nomination for leading role in "Leaf Blower" (2014)
- **Comedy Expertise**: Stand-up comedian and creator of theatrical show "El Baúl de Cuentos Mágicos"
- **Streaming Success**: Starring role in Amazon Prime's hit series "How to Survive Being Single"

## 🎯 Design Philosophy

This website embodies the essence of cinematic storytelling:

1. **Visual Hierarchy**: Like a movie poster, the most important information is presented prominently
2. **Emotional Journey**: The user experience mirrors the arc of a compelling film
3. **Attention to Detail**: Every element, from typography to spacing, serves the narrative
4. **Cultural Authenticity**: Bilingual content that honors Mexican cinema traditions
5. **Professional Polish**: Befitting an actor of Fabrizio's caliber and industry standing
